Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Face Buddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Display name, age range, and biological sex (used to personalize your experience and improve the accuracy of AI-generated transformation previews).
  • Assessment Data: Health and lifestyle information you provide during onboarding, including breathing habits, jaw alignment, and facial health goals.
  • Facial Photos: Photos captured through the App for progress tracking and AI analysis. These are taken from multiple angles (front, profile, smile).
  • Progress Notes: Any notes you add to your weekly progress entries.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics data about how you interact with the App, including exercise completions, streak milestones, and feature usage, to improve our service.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version, used for compatibility and troubleshooting.
  • Subscription Status: Transaction identifiers related to in-app purchases, managed through Apple's App Store.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Calendar data
  • Browsing history
  • Financial or payment card information (payments are processed entirely by Apple)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core features, including AI-powered facial analysis and exercise recommendations.
  • Generate transformation preview images based on your photos and profile.
  • Track your progress over time by comparing weekly photos to your baseline.
  • Personalize your exercise routine based on your assessment and goals.
  • Send you notifications and reminders (with your permission).
  • Improve and optimize the App based on anonymized usage analytics.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with the following categories of service providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • AI Processing Services: Your photos are sent to third-party AI providers (such as Google and OpenAI) for analysis and transformation generation. These providers process your data solely to provide results to you through the App and are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Analytics Providers: We use anonymized analytics services to understand usage patterns and improve the App.
  • Infrastructure Providers: Cloud hosting and database services that store your data securely.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Your facial photos are stored securely and are only accessible to you through the App. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile section in the App. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Notifications: You can manage notification preferences within the App or through your device settings.
  • Camera Access: You can revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings. The App requires camera access to capture progress photos.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
